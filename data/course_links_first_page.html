Course Description
This course focuses on fundamental concepts of financial reporting and the economics of financial markets
Course Learning Outcomes (CLOs)
  1. 1. Read, analyze, and interpret financial accounting information.
  2. 2. Use financial accounting information in making informed business decisions.

Class Attendance Attendance at all class sessions is required. Failure to attend a class session, late arrival, or early departure may result in lowering of your final grade or failure of the course due to non-attendance. Questions or concerns must be addressed to your TA. Communication with the instructor and the TAs The instructor and the TAs are available to answer questions related to the course material. The best way to contact the instructor will be to see him during office hours. Students can also e-mail the instructor and the TAs with short questions, writing in the subject line of all e-mail correspondence “Financial Accounting — UG — Name of the Student”. Please note that it is difficult to discuss technical or complex questions by e-mail due to the lack of an appropriate interface. Hence, students are encouraged to ask such questions during office hours
